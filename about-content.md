# About Flikax

## Buy. Sell. Discover.

Flikax is a modern online classifieds marketplace that makes it easy for people to buy, sell, rent, and discover products and services in their local communities. Whether you're clearing out unused items, growing your business, searching for your next car, renting a property, or looking for trusted services, Flikax connects you with real buyers and sellers through a simple, secure, and user-friendly platform.

Our mission is to make local commerce faster, safer, and more accessible for everyone.

# How to Sell on Flikax

### 1. Create an Account

Register using your email address or sign in with Google.

### 2. Post Your Listing

Upload quality photos, choose the appropriate category, write a clear title and description, enter your price, and publish your advert.

### 3. Receive Enquiries

Interested buyers can contact you through Flikax.

### 4. Complete Your Sale

Arrange a safe meeting place, inspect the item together, receive payment, and complete the transaction.

---

# How to Buy on Flikax

### 1. Search

Browse categories or search using keywords.

### 2. Compare Listings

Review prices, photos, seller information, and descriptions before making a decision.

### 3. Contact the Seller

Ask questions, negotiate where appropriate, and arrange a meeting.

### 4. Buy Safely

Inspect the item before making payment and complete the transaction in a secure public location whenever possible.

# Safety Comes First

At Flikax, we're committed to creating a trusted marketplace. While we work hard to detect fraudulent activity and moderate listings, every user also plays an important role in keeping the community safe.

We encourage all buyers and sellers to:

* Meet in safe public places.
* Inspect items before paying.
* Never send money in advance unless you completely trust the seller.
* Report suspicious listings or users.
* Keep communication respectful and honest.

Visit our **Trust & Safety** page to learn more.
